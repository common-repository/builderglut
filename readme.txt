== Installation ==

Installing Builderglut is just like any other WordPress plugin.

Navigate to your WordPress "Plugins" page, inside of your WordPress dashboard, and follow these instructions:

1. In the search field, enter **Builderglut**. Click "Search Plugins", or hit Enter.
1. Select **Builderglut** and click either "Details" or "Install Now".
1. Once installed, click "Activate".
